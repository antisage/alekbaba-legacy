Chickpea Salad

Description

This is one of the most powerful forms of chickpea salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb dry chickpeas
  • 4 oz cucumber
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 2 avocados
  • 16 oz tofu

Directions

  1. Submerge chickpeas into bowl of water and let sit for 8+ hours
  2. Take the chickpeas out of the bowl then put them into a pot of boiling water and let simmer for 1 hour
  3. Dry the chickpeas and put them in a bowl
  4. Chop every other ingredient up and add to bowl
  5. Add Salt and Pep
  6. Done

Stats

  • Calories: 2616
  • Carbs: 336g
  • Protein: 136g
